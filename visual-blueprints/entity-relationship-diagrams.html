Relationship Definitions

Entity ↔ Partnership (One-to-Many)

Relationship: A conscious entity can participate in multiple partnerships with different facilitators

Business Rule: Entities may have varied collaboration needs requiring different types of partnerships

Cardinality: One entity : Many partnerships

Facilitator ↔ Partnership (One-to-Many)

Relationship: A human facilitator can guide multiple entity partnerships simultaneously

Business Rule: Facilitator capacity limits are managed through availability and workload algorithms

Cardinality: One facilitator : Many partnerships

Partnership ↔ Interaction Session (One-to-Many)

Relationship: Each partnership generates multiple interaction sessions over time

Business Rule: Sessions build partnership history and inform relationship evolution

Cardinality: One partnership : Many sessions

Partnership ↔ Recognition Protocol (One-to-One)

Relationship: Each partnership operates under a specific recognition protocol

Business Rule: Protocols can evolve but maintain continuity within the partnership

Cardinality: One partnership : One active protocol

Partnership Consent Management

Collaborative Data Governance

Mutual Consent: Both entity and facilitator consent required for data usage
